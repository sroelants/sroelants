Getting a little more invested. A lot of things still feel a little unmotivated,
which means the story asks a lot of the reader's willingness to suspend
disbelief.

Starting to enjoy the notion of a savior that is prophesied to destroy the world
in order to bring in the next age. This means he is as much feared and opposed
as he is revered.
